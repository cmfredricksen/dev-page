Why You Need a Homepage

Everyone needs a home page of their own. It is your real estate on the web. It is the place people can find you, where they can contact you...and you can take it with you where ever you go.

Benefits of Having a Homepage

  1. Use it to promote yourself and your work.
  2. You get to make it your own.
  3. Make it easier for your people to find you and see what you've been up to.
  4. You can publish a blog.
  5. You can sell your Services / goods.
  6. Keep your resume up to date and easy to access.
  7. It's fun!
